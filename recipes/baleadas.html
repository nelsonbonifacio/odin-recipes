Baleadas

Description

The most common meal in Honduras and my personal favorite meal are Baleadas. Baleadas is a dish made by layering refried beans, cheese, sour cream, and more in a tortilla.

ingredients

  • refried beans
  • cheese
  • sour cream
  1. Get or Make a flour tortilla
  2. Add beans
  3. Add cheese
  4. Add sour cream
  5. Add any additional topping you want
